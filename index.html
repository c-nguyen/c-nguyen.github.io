Graphic/Web Design

I was first introduced to graphic design when I stumbled onto honkkiat.com. The artwork on there amazed me. I was intrigued and wanted to know how to create these by myself. I started learning Adobe Photoshop and continue to learn new things. While learning Photoshop, I also started learning HTML and CSS.

Girl Scouts

I became a Girl Scout the summer of 2009. I joined Troop 997: Lien Doan Hoa Lu. I started at the bottom of the ranks, and slowly moved up the ranks. I got received the highest honor, which is the Gold Award. Now, I am a certified, assistant troop leader. I'm the head of over 60 girls. I wanted to stay with my troop after receiving my Gold Award because Girl Scouts helped me break out of my shell. I was a very shy person, but now I am more comfortable talking to other people and have made many new friendships. I wanted to help other Girl Scouts see the amazing opportunities Girl Scouting creates for them.
