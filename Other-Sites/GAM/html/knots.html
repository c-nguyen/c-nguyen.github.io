Knots

Bowline

To make a loop in a line that will never slip, jam, or fail.

Cleat Hitch

To secure a line to a cleat.

Clove Hitch

To temporarily fasten a line around a spar or pile.

Figure Eight

To keep a line from running through the end of a block or fairlead.

Fisherman's Knot

To tie two thin lines of the same thickness together.

Round Turn & Two Half Hitches

To make a line fast to a mooring.

Sheet Bend

To tie 2 lines together of unequal diameter together so that they will stay tied.

Square Knot/Reef Knot

To tie two lines of the same thickness together.

Taut Line

To make a loop in a line that will slide but not slip.

Timber Hitch

To tow or hoist a pole or spar.
